Geo Vermonter 
This is a game where you try to guess the county you are in, 
                and then the town. You start with a score 
                of 20, for each time you scroll the map
                 screen north, south, east or west you 
                 are deducted 1 point. You are also deducted 
                 one point whenever you guess the county or town wrong. 
                 Hit start to be placed at a random point within Vermont.
